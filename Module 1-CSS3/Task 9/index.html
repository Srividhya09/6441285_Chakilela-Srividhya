Community News Bulletin

The local community center is launching several new initiatives this month including free yoga classes, weekend art workshops, and senior citizen digital literacy sessions. Residents are encouraged to participate and give feedback.

Volunteers are also needed for the upcoming charity run and food distribution drive. If interested, please register at the information desk or through our online portal.

In other news, the council has approved new funding for park improvements and street lighting in residential areas. Stay tuned for the detailed schedule of development activities in the next town hall meeting.
